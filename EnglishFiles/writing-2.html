The hippo

    Once upon a time, there was a hippo, he loved bananas. One day the hippo was happy, he had just found a banana! Happily, he held his banana and swung from tree to tree. He liked thinking of himself as a monkey, in fact, he was the most athletic hippo around! Today, he was just minding his own business and acting like a monkey, when all of a sudden he heard giggles. He turned around to see monkeys laughing at him! "You'll never be a monkey!", they laughed, "You'll never be athletic enough, you will always remain a hippo!". Now, the hippo wasn't going to let his dream slide, to prove himself, he challenged the monkeys to a duel, if he won, he would be a real monkey!
    The day quickly arrived, which the hippo trained hard for. The tournament started with both sides throwing sticks. All of a sudden, a stick flies toward the hippo. He was impaled by the stick and died.
