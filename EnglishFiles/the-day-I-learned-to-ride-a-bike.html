The day I learned to ride a bike

    I can't remember the day I learned to ride a bike, because it was many days. I can remember having training wheels on the bike, and then having it taken off. I fell down and scraped my knees alot, but I persevered through it. Before I knew, I was speeding along the wind, if felt good to have the feeling of going anywhere. Although I can't think of a specific day, I will always remember the feeling of riding the bike!
